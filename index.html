Since November 13, 1984, the TMA Food Pantry has been “Feeding Hunger with Heart.” Hundreds of people in Taylorville receive nutritional food assitence from us, and we get donated by other stores and many individual donars.

Through our partnership with the Senior Citizens of Christian Country, round-trip transportation to the Food Pantry is available on the second and fourth Thursday nights at 5:30 pm from Hershey Tower and Saturday mornings at 8:30 am from Park Glen and Senior Village Apartments at 8:45 am.
